verboseIter
  verbose iterator from vector 
 	verbose iterator constructed from vector. The verbose iterator automatically displays and updates a waitbar
 
 	Example:
 	v = verboseIter([1, 2, 7, 8])
 	while v.hasNext()
 		[s, i] = v.next();
 	end
 	v.close();
