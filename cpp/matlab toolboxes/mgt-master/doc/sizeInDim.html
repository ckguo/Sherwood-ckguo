sizeInDim.m
  size of volume in a space of dimension dim (where dim >= ndims(vol)). So if the volume is less
  dimensions that dim, the size will be appended with ones.
 
  Example:
    r = rand(5, 5);
    sizeInDim(r, 4)
    ans =
           5     5     1     1
